CoinOS

CoinOS is an open-source and completely FREE Point of Sale system built by co-op members. It provides merchants with a simple to use system for taking bitcoin payments and managing administration.

CoinOS works in any Internet browser, so can be used with any iPhone or Android tablet, and can be branded with your logo. It is simple to use, making staff training a doddle!

Website: www.coinos.io

Dignitas International

Dignitas International is one of the most renowned charitable organizations to accept Bitcoin, having gathered millions of dollars worth of donations and produced over 25 research papers. By lowering transaction fees for both donations and overseas transfer, they hope Bitcoin can help them prevent and treat AIDS in Africa.

Website: http://dignitasinternational.org/
