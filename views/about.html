About Us

The Bitcoin Co-op is a democratic, voluntary association of individuals united by a mutual interest in bitcoin, a revolutionary digital currency and payment technology. We support our members by negotiating discounts and deals with participating merchants, offering discounted rates on exchange, and supporting businesses that accept bitcoins by consulting with them on payment processing services and promoting them through our network. We host regular events, meetups, hackathons and info sessions to bring people to bitcoin-friendly venues, build community, and raise awareness about the technology.

We are proud to be the Canadian integration partner for bitpay.com, the premier payment processing company for bitcoin. They enable businesses to accept bitcoins for payment and automatically convert them into dollars via direct deposit into Canadian bank accounts.

BitPay charges a 1% transaction fee in return for their currency exchange services. We collect half of that fee from any payments to merchants that sign up with BitPay through our affiliate URL: https://bitpay.com/merchant-signup?oid=1035_1

Members of the co-operative are eligible to receive 60% of the transaction fees we receive from merchants that they refer to BitPay through our program.
